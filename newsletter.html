Who Are We?

This website was created for a school project by a Liverpool fan. It's purpose is to inform and entertain the user about Liverpool's history and legacy as a club. The events described as "Liverpool's greatest moments" are based off of the opinion of the designer. While this site may link to legitimate Liverpool's websites, this site itself is not associated with Liverpool FC in any way. "Joining the newsletter" will not actually sign you up for anything. Thanks for checking out the website and I hope you enjoyed!
